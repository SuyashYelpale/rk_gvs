Our Journey

Founded in 2010 in Pimpri, Maharashtra, Radha Krishna Gram Vikas Sanstha (RKGVS) has been dedicated to uplifting rural communities through sustainable development programs.

Guided by spiritual values and practical solutions, we work hand-in-hand with villages to create lasting change in education, healthcare, and economic stability.
